About our school

Specialized state comprehensive school No. 300 of Sergeli district of Tashkent city began its activity in 1995. President Shavkat Mirziyoyev awarded Saidkhodjaeva Nigora Ergashevna, the director of school No. 300 of Sergeli district of Tashkent city, the honorary title of "People's Teacher of the Republic of Uzbekistan". Currently, 8514 students study at the school. Instruction is conducted in Russian and Uzbek languages ​​in two shifts. A total of 152 teachers teach at the school. Of these, 6 teachers work in the highest category, 17 teachers in the first category and 46 teachers in the second category.
